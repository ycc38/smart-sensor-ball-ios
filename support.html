How to Get Help

Email 869501402@qq.com for app support. Include your app version, iPhone model, iOS version, Bluetooth device name, and screenshots if possible.

Bluetooth Device Connection

Open the app settings page, allow Bluetooth permission, scan for a device named SENBALL# or an FFE0 BLE device, and connect before starting training.

Activation and Cloud Sync

The activation code verifies that the user owns or is authorized to use the companion physical Smart sensor ball hardware. Activation does not sell digital content in the app.

Before training scores are uploaded to cloud sync or global leaderboards, the app asks for consent. Users can decline and continue local training.

Data Requests

For correction, deletion, or removal from leaderboard display, contact zclei@vip.sina.com.
